Design Project

(Please know this is a ficticious event designed solely for the interview process)

Please prepare an 8.5x11 design in presentation format using the following information:

You have been asked to design a concept for the Iowa Hawk Shop’s “Be Bold, Wear Gold” campaign. Each week will focus on a product that will be pushed and offered at a discount for that week. This campaign will include advertisements, flyers, in-store signage, displays, and web graphics. For this assignment, please design the 8.5”x11” flyer.

The design should capture the spirit and excitement of the Hawkeye football season. The client has emphasized their desire to incorporate the school colors of black and gold (Pantone 116).

The first week’s special is 20% off all gold Nike products.

Text provided by the client:

  • “Be Bold, Wear Gold!”
  • 20% off all gold Nike products
  • Offer good August 20-26, 2012
  • Old Capitol Mall, 201 S. Clinton St.
  • www.HAWKSHOP.com, 1-800-HAWK SHOP
  • while supplies last, offer good in store and online.
